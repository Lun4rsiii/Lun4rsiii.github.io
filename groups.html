Archons !

The Archons of Genshin Impact are the seven divine rulers presiding over the nations of Teyvat, each embodying an element and a specific ideal. They are gods who won the Archon War 2,000 years ago, earning a "Gnosis" from Celestia to rule, and are distinct from other gods by their seat among "The Seven". Though over the course of there rule, some have fallen and successors have taken there place (I will show the ones that are currently in the game...)

The Hexenzirkel

The Hexenzirkel are an elite, mysterious coven of powerful, magically gifted women in Genshin Impact who explore Teyvat, study Irminsul, and host "tea parties" to discuss metaphysics. The purpose, motives and objectives of the Hexenzirkel are shrouded in mystery. The coven is comprised of some of the most powerful beings in Teyvat, with some possessing abilities that rival even the gods. While never being explicitly cast in either a benevolent or antagonistic light, members of the Hexenzirkel have nonetheless been implicated in matters of world-changing significance, such as the Cataclysm

The Fatui

The Fatui are a delegation of diplomats from the Zapolyarny Palace of Snezhnaya. There is also a military detachment led by the Eleven Fatui Harbingers and the Tsaritsa herself. They are one of the two main antagonistic factions alongside the Abyss Order in Genshin Impact and the main antagonists in the official supplementary manga.
